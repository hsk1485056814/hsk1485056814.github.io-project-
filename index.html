Hi! I'm Shengkun Huang, a student at Hunan University of Arts and Science, majoring in Geographic Information Science (GIS).
To me, GIS is more than just a tool; it is a vital bridge connecting macroscopic landscapes with microscopic data. My academic focus lies at the intersection of Remote Sensing and Human Geography. To broaden my academic horizons and prepare for advanced scholarly challenges, I am also dedicated to mastering Japanese..

Here, I share my professional notes, competition experiences, and every step of my journey in the world of GIS.
